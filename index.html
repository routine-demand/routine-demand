Routine Demand Log

Welcome to a public experiment for recurring essential needs. This is a small-scale experiment to record items you know you will need regularly, such as daily or office supplies.

What this is

This is a tool to record your future recurring needs. It is not a shopping site, and no orders will be taken or fulfilled.

Why we do this

  • Many small offices or teams repeatedly purchase the same items.
  • Suppliers cannot always predict demand accurately.
  • This experiment collects anonymous recurring needs to see patterns.

Who should participate

Small offices, studios, or teams that have recurring purchases for essentials.

Important Notice / Disclaimer

  • We do not sell or deliver any items.
  • No transactions or payments will be processed through this page.
  • Filling the form does not create any obligation or guarantee of fulfillment.
  • All information is collected for experiment purposes only.
